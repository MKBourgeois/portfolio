Post Graduate Program in Generative AI for Business Applications  ·  January–April 2026
The "AI April" challenges show what's possible in hours with the right tools. The certification program shows what it takes to understand why they work — and when they don't. Both are part of the same story.
Why This Program — and Why it Mmatters Here

This program isn't about creating a career pivot. My goal was never to become a data engineer or build medical AI agents. It was to develop a firsthand understanding of how these systems actually work — so I can design better enablement deliverables, evaluate AI tools with real judgment, and build prototypes that solve real problems.

This program has made me a more credible partner for working with AI vendors, technical SMEs, dev tools, and product teams. It's given me the ability to assess what LLMs can and can't do responsibly — hallucinations, grounding, over and under fitting when training, prompt design, agentic risk — which is increasingly critical as AI reshapes how partners learn and sell.

Understanding the technology from the inside makes me a better thought partner — and a more useful bridge between what's technically possible and what actually serves our audience.
